UX Case - Design for a Healthy recipe Book mobile app

  • by:Shivani Singh
  • date:December, 2022
  • UX, UI, Research, priorization
This is an individual project that I made during the UX-UI Master cource.

The Challange

Striking the right balance between a visually appealing design and a functional, distraction-free layout. Overly artistic layouts can compromise readability or navigation.

The Solution

Prioritize clean, minimalistic designs with a focus on typography and white space. Use high-quality, appetizing images without overloading screens. Maintain consistent layouts across pages for a predictable user experience.

objective

To create a user-friendly, visually appealing, and intuitive Healthy Food Recipe App that caters to users with varying dietary preferences, cooking skills, and health goals. The app includes a Login Screen, Profile Setup Screen, and Home Screen featuring categories like meals, salads, desserts, and healthy food, along with detailed ingredients and preparation steps for each recipe.

Introduction

Onboarding Screen

Purpose:The login screen serves as the gateway to the app, ensuring a personalized user experience while keeping user data secure. Design Features:Minimalistic Layout: Background: Soft, gradient tones or a high-quality food-related image for a welcoming feel. App Logo: Placed prominently at the top for brand recall. Login Options: Email and password input fields.Social login buttons: Google, Apple, and Facebook for quicker access. Clear "Forgot Password?" link for easy recovery. Call-to-Action Buttons: "Log In" and "Sign Up" buttons styled with vibrant colors (e.g., green or orange) to signify health and action. Onboarding Shortcut: Optional “Explore Without Login” button for first-time users to browse limited features. Key UX Considerations: Auto-focus on the email field when the screen loads. Error messages with friendly prompts (e.g., "Please enter a valid email address").

Outcome

By focusing on clean, engaging design elements and prioritizing user preferences, the Healthy Food Recipe App ensures a positive user experience. The seamless integration of ingredients and preparation details supports new and experienced cooks alike, fostering confidence and ease in the kitchen.
